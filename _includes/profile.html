Services descriptions

R Programming & Data Analysis

Since 2015 I've been working with R, making several courses and helping in data management in the Experimental Ecology Laboratory (Simón Bolívar University). Thanks to that, I've learn how to deal with a relatively large amount of data, how to build R functions and how to implement graphics and statistical analysis as needed.

Experimental Design

I have studied and applied concepts of statistical analysis and experimental design. The critical reading of a project is an important part of science, as it is to be capable of making the right recomendations about an experiment's design or the posterior statistical analysis.

Spatial Analysis

For the past two years I've learned how to use and build a GIS using ArcGIS. Also, I've learned how to manage spatial files, like .shp, in R to make changes and quick filters. For the past year I've been working on the development of a web map server using ArcGIS online platform for the project "Conservation of Biodiversity in the Orinoco's Oil Belt", as part of LSIGMA (Geographical Information System and Environmental Modeling Laboratory - Simón Bolívar University)

Text Mining and Analysis

During my communitary service (a 120 hours requisite to obtain Bachelor's Degree in Venezuela), I worked for Provita , an NGO dedicated to biodiversity conservation in Venezuela. At this time I learned how to use several R tools for web scrapping, data mining and text mining. Specially I developed some R scripts adjusted to download an organize Twitter data by searched topic, subtopic, date, and geographical coordinates, as well as other parameters requested by Twitter's API. This can be replicated to other social networks like Instagram, Facebook, Tumblr, etc.

Fish Stomachal Content Analysis

For my grade project (undergraduate thesis - requisite for Bachelor's degree), I researched if there was spacial variability associated with the diets of demersal fishes and trophic webs in the marine-estuarine region of the Orinoco's River Delta. In this project I learned how to make stomachal content analysis, how to construct food webs in R, how to make extinsion simulations on those food webs, and many other things.

Identification of Coral Reef Benthic Substrate

For the past 8 months, we at the Experimental Ecology Laboratory have been working with reef benthic surface analysis, identifying benthic organisms like cnidarians, sponges, echinoderms, algaes, etc. These projects are being financed by the Waitt Institue. The majority of the images correspond to different caribbean reefs, from Venezuela, Barbuda, Monserrat, etc. We have also learned how to identify corals of the pacific Ocean, mainly from the Kingdom of Tonga.
