I really enjoy studying and learning about the world that surround us. Specially, I think that marine and other aquatic ecosystems are amazing and need to be studied and protected from human impacts and climate change.

The more time we dedicate to comprehend these ecosystems, its interactions with the surrounding environment, organisms, and us, the faster we will be able to offer solutions that will help the world to have a sustainable and healtier development.

My Services
R programming &
Data Analysis

Statistical analysis using R. Creation of functions or subrutines to analyse data. Use of graphical libraries like "ggplot2" package.

Experimental design

Knowledge of experimental design and descriptive, inferential and predictive statistics.

Spatial analysis

Knowledge of GIS, using R, ArcGIS and the web platform ArcGIS online.

Text mining and analysis

Knowledge about data mining techniques and text analysis, primarily from social networks like Twitter.

Fish stomachal content analysis

Taxonomic identification of fishes, crustaceans, moluscs, and other animal or vegetal groups that can be found within a fish stomachs. Diet analysis and food web analysis.

Identification of Coral Reef Benthic Substrate

Identification of corals and associated benthic organisms, both in the Caribbean sea and Pacific Ocean.
